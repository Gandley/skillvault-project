Refund Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how SkillVault handles refunds for digital products. By making a purchase on SkillVault, you acknowledge that you are buying digital goods and agree to the terms outlined below.

💳 Pay-Per-Skill

$9 One-Time Skill Purchases

General policy: All Pay-Per-Skill purchases are final. Because skills are digital files that can be downloaded and used immediately, we do not offer refunds once a skill has been downloaded.

Exception — no download: If you purchased a skill but have not downloaded the file, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase. We verify this in our system before processing the refund.

Exception — technical failure: If the skill file is corrupted, incomplete, or fundamentally non-functional (not due to user error), contact support within 14 days. We will either:

  • Replace the file with a working version
  • Issue a full refund if replacement is not possible

Not eligible for refund: Change of mind, skill not meeting subjective expectations, or skills downloaded but unused. We provide detailed descriptions and preview metadata to help you make informed purchases.

👑 Vault Pro Subscription

$27/Month Subscription

Free trial / grace period: You may cancel within 7 days of your initial subscription start date for a full refund, no questions asked. This applies to first-time subscribers only.

Monthly billing: After the 7-day grace period, subscriptions are billed monthly and are non-refundable for partial months. If you cancel mid-month, your access continues until the end of the current billing cycle.

Cancellation: You can cancel anytime from your account dashboard or by contacting support. Cancellation stops the next billing cycle; you retain access until the paid period ends.

Pro-rated refunds: We do not offer pro-rated refunds for unused days in a billing period. The monthly fee covers unlimited access to all skills during that period, regardless of usage.

Exception — platform failure: If SkillVault experiences extended downtime or a material reduction in service quality (e.g., skills cannot be downloaded for 48+ hours due to our fault), we may issue a credit or refund at our discretion.

🎁 Free Skills

Free Tier Downloads

Free skills are available at no cost. No refunds apply as no payment is collected. Free skills are provided "as-is" with community support.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Email support@vaultofskills.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account email address and the skill name or subscription date
  3. State the reason for your refund request
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for review and processing

Refunds are processed to the original payment method. Stripe typically completes refunds within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.

Fraud Prevention

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in cases of suspected fraud, including:

  • Multiple refund requests from the same account in a short period
  • Refund requests immediately after downloading multiple skills
  • Accounts with patterns consistent with abuse of the refund system

Fraudulent refund attempts may result in account suspension and legal action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Refund requests are evaluated based on the policy in effect at the time of purchase.
