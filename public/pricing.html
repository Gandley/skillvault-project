Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in Vault Pro?

Vault Pro gives you unlimited access to all 18 current skills plus every new skill we release. New skills are added monthly, and Pro members get them immediately at no extra cost. You also get priority support, advanced documentation with real-world examples, and early access to beta features before they go public.

Can I buy skills individually?

Yes. 5 premium skills are available as one-time purchases for $9 each. Once you buy a skill, it's yours forever — no recurring fees, no expiration. You'll need a free SkillVault account to manage your purchases and downloads. Individual skills are perfect if you only need specific tools rather than the full library.

How do I access my downloads after purchase?

After any purchase, your skills are available in your SkillVault dashboard. Free skills download after logging in. Paid and Pro skills require logging into your account — from there you can download, update, and reinstall anytime. All files are yours to keep and use across unlimited projects.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Absolutely. Vault Pro is a month-to-month subscription with no contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel anytime from your account settings, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. If you rejoin later, your access and any previously downloaded skills are restored immediately.

What's the difference between Free and Paid skills?

Free skills are our most beginner-friendly tools — simple to set up, well-documented, and production-ready. Paid skills ($9) are more advanced, with deeper integrations, more features, and broader use cases. Pro skills are our most powerful offerings, including cutting-edge AI features, automation workflows, and complex integrations. All tiers are built to the same quality standard.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If a skill doesn't meet your expectations, contact our support team within 14 days and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked. Pro subscriptions can also be refunded within the first 14 days of any billing cycle.
